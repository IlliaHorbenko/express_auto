Why import a car from the USA?

We compared the US markets with European ones and realized that buying a car in America is several times more profitable, no matter how paradoxical it may sound. This is due to thoughtful logistics, the level of development of services for assessing the condition of a car and the process of buying a car itself.

Most US citizens take a car on lease for several years and the leasing company itself is engaged in constant maintenance of the car during the entire period of operation, as a result, cars from the USA are one of the best choices for car enthusiasts in Ukraine.

Why such a low price?

Damaged cars from the USA are redeemed from auctions of insurance companies. At these auctions, the car loses half the price even due to minimal damage. If we take into account the cash costs, namely the ransom, delivery, customs and repairs, then the price of a car similar in condition in Ukraine will be 35-50% higher, and new ones will cost space money.
